Phone Mechanic Parramatta

We are located at Westfield Parramatta, on the Argyle Street front. Here's how you can find us:

  1. Go to Level 1 of the food court and locate KFC.
  2. Opposite KFC, you will find an escalator.
  3. Take the escalator and exit through the main entrance of Westfield.
  4. Once you're on Argyle Street, turn left.
  5. You'll find us right after the barber shop (By Jaime STYLES).
